THA AKAMPANEEING PROWGRAHM IZ PRAVIYDAD ANDER THA TERMZ AV THIS IKLIPS PABLIK
LIYSANS ("AGREEMANT"). ENEE YOOS, REEPRADAKSHAN OUR DISTRABYOOSHAN AV THA PROWGRAHM
KONSTATOOTS risipeeants AHKSEPTANS AV THIS AGREEMANT.

1. DEFANISHANZ

"Kontrabyooshan" meenz:

a) in tha kais av tha inishal Kantribyater, tha inishal kowd and
dokyamentaishan distribyatad ander this Agreemant, and

bee) in tha kais av eech sabsakwant Kantribyater:

iy) chainjaz too tha Prowgrahm, and

ii) adishanz too tha Prowgrahm;

wer sach chainjaz and/our adishanz too tha Prowgrahm erijanait fram and or
distribyatad biy thaht pertikyaler Kantribyater. A Kontrabyooshan 'originates' fram
a Kantribyater if it woz ahdid too tha Prowgrahm biy sach Kantribyater itself our
eneewan ahkting on sach Contributor's bihahf. Kontrabyooshanz doo not inklood
adishanz too tha Prowgrahm wich: (iy) or seperait mojoolz av souftwer
distribyatad in kanjangkshan with tha Prowgrahm ander ther own liysans
agreemant, and (ii) or not derivativ werks av tha Prowgrahm.

"Kantribyater" meenz enee persan our entatee thaht distribyoots tha Prowgrahm.

"Liysanst Pahtants" meen pahtant klaimz licensable biy a Kantribyater wich or
nesaseralee infrinjd biy tha yoos our sail av its Kontrabyooshan alown our wen
kambiynd with tha Prowgrahm.

"Prowgrahm" meenz tha Kontrabyooshanz distribyatad in akourdans with this
Agreemant.

"Rasipeeant" meenz eneewan hoo raseevz tha Prowgrahm ander this Agreemant,
inklooding oul Kantribyaterz.

2. GRAHNT AV RIYTS

a) Sabjekt too tha termz av this Agreemant, eech Kantribyater hirbiy grahnts
Rasipeeant a non-ikskloosiv, werldwiyd, roialtee-free kopeeriyt liysans too
reepradoos, preeper derivativ werks av, pabliklee displai, pabliklee perfourm,
distribyoot and sublicense tha Kontrabyooshan av sach Kantribyater, if enee, and
sach derivativ werks, in sours kowd and objekt kowd fourm.

bee) Sabjekt too tha termz av this Agreemant, eech Kantribyater hirbiy grahnts
Rasipeeant a non-ikskloosiv, werldwiyd, roialtee-free pahtant liysans ander
Liysanst Pahtants too maik, yoos, sel, oufer too sel, impourt and atherwiyz
trahnsfer tha Kontrabyooshan av sach Kantribyater, if enee, in sours kowd and
objekt kowd fourm.  This pahtant liysans shahl apliy too tha kombanaishan av tha
Kontrabyooshan and tha Prowgrahm if, aht tha tiym tha Kontrabyooshan iz ahdid biy tha
Kantribyater, sach adishan av tha Kontrabyooshan kozaz sach kombanaishan too bee
kaverd biy tha Liysanst Pahtants. Tha pahtant liysans shahl not apliy too enee
ather kombanaishanz wich inklood tha Kontrabyooshan. Now hordwer per sai iz
liysanst hereunder.

see) Rasipeeant anderstahndz thaht oulthow eech Kantribyater grahnts tha liysansiz
too its Kontrabyooshanz set fourtth hirin, now ashuuransiz or praviydad biy enee
Kantribyater thaht tha Prowgrahm daz not infrinj tha pahtant our ather
intalekchooal propertee riyts av enee ather entatee. Eech Kantribyater disklaimz
enee liyabilitee too Rasipeeant four klaimz brout biy enee ather entatee baist on
infrinjmant av intalekchooal propertee riyts our atherwiyz. Ahz a kandishan too
eksersiyzing tha riyts and liysansiz grahntad hereunder, eech Rasipeeant hirbiy
asoomz sowl reesponsabilatee too sikyuur enee ather intalekchooal propertee riyts
needad, if enee. Four igzahmpal, if a ttherd portee pahtant liysans iz reekwiyerd too
alow Rasipeeant too distribyoot tha Prowgrahm, it iz risipeeants reesponsabilatee
too akwiyer thaht liysans bifour distribyooting tha Prowgrahm.

dee) Eech Kantribyater reprazents thaht too its nolaj it hahz safishant
kopeeriyt riyts in its Kontrabyooshan, if enee, too grahnt tha kopeeriyt liysans
set fourtth in this Agreemant.

3. RIKWIYRMANTS

A Kantribyater mai chooz too distribyoot tha Prowgrahm in objekt kowd fourm ander
its own liysans agreemant, praviydad thaht:

a) it kampliyz with tha termz and kandishanz av this Agreemant; and

bee) its liysans agreemant:

iy) ifektivlee disklaimz on bihahf av oul Kantribyaterz oul wouranteez and
kandishanz, ikspres and impliyd, inklooding wouranteez our kandishanz av tiytal
and non-infrinjmant, and impliyd wouranteez our kandishanz av merchantability
and fitnas four a pertikyaler perpas;

ii) ifektivlee ikskloodz on bihahf av oul Kantribyaterz oul liyabilitee four
dahmajaz, inklooding derekt, inderekt, speshal, insidental and konsakwenshal
dahmajaz, sach ahz loust profits;

iii) staits thaht enee pravisianz wich difer fram this Agreemant or ouferd
biy thaht Kantribyater alown and not biy enee ather portee; and

iv) staits thaht sours kowd four tha Prowgrahm iz availabal fram sach
Kantribyater, and infourmz liysanseez how too abtain it in a reezanabal mahner on
our tthroo a meedeeam kastameralee yoozd four souftwer ikschainj.

Wen tha Prowgrahm iz maid availabal in sours kowd fourm:

a) it mast bee maid availabal ander this Agreemant; and

bee) a kopee av this Agreemant mast bee inkloodad with eech kopee av tha Prowgrahm.

Kantribyaterz mai not reemoov our oulter enee kopeeriyt nowtisiz kantaind within
tha Prowgrahm.

Eech Kantribyater mast iydentafiy itself ahz tha erijanaiter av its Kontrabyooshan,
if enee, in a mahner thaht reezanablee alowz sabsakwant Risipeeants too iydentafiy
tha erijanaiter av tha Kontrabyooshan.

4. KAMERSHAL DISTRABYOOSHAN

Kamershal distribyaterz av souftwer mai ahksept sertan reesponsabilateez with
rispekt too end yoozerz, biznas portnerz and tha liyk. Wiyl this liysans iz
intendid too fasilatait tha kamershal yoos av tha Prowgrahm, tha Kantribyater hoo
inkloodz tha Prowgrahm in a kamershal prodakt oufering shuud doo sow in a
mahner wich daz not kreeait patenshal liyabilitee four ather Kantribyaterz.
Therfour, if a Kantribyater inkloodz tha Prowgrahm in a kamershal prodakt
oufering, sach Kantribyater ("Kamershal Kantribyater") hirbiy agreez too difend
and indemnafiy everee ather Kantribyater ("Indemnafiyd Kantribyater") agenst enee
lousaz, dahmajaz and kosts (kalektivlee "Lousaz") eriyzing fram klaimz,
lousoots and ather leegal ahkshanz brout biy a ttherd portee agenst tha
Indemnafiyd Kantribyater too tha ikstent kozd biy tha ahkts our owmishanz av sach
Kamershal Kantribyater in kanekshan with its distrabyooshan av tha Prowgrahm in
a kamershal prodakt oufering.  Tha oblagaishanz in this sekshan doo not apliy
too enee klaimz our Lousaz rilaiting too enee ahkchawal our alejd intalekchooal
propertee infrinjmant. In ourder too kwolafiy, ahn Indemnafiyd Kantribyater mast:
a) promptlee nowtafiy tha Kamershal Kantribyater in riyting av sach klaim, and
bee) alow tha Kamershal Kantribyater tocontrol, and kowoperait with tha
Kamershal Kantribyater in, tha difens and enee rilaitid setalmant
nagowsheeaishanz. Tha Indemnafiyd Kantribyater mai portisapait in enee sach klaim
aht its own ikspens.

Four igzahmpal, a Kantribyater miyt inklood tha Prowgrahm in a kamershal prodakt
oufering, Prodakt EKS. Thaht Kantribyater iz then a Kamershal Kantribyater. If
thaht Kamershal Kantribyater then maiks perfourmans klaimz, our ouferz
wouranteez rilaitid too Prodakt EKS, thowz perfourmans klaimz and wouranteez or
sach Kamershal Contributor's reesponsabilatee alown. Ander this sekshan, tha
Kamershal Kantribyater wuud hahv too difend klaimz agenst tha ather
Kantribyaterz rilaitid too thowz perfourmans klaimz and wouranteez, and if a
kourt reekwiyerz enee ather Kantribyater too pai enee dahmajaz ahz a rizalt, tha
Kamershal Kantribyater mast pai thowz dahmajaz.

5. NOW WOURANTEE

IKSEPT AHZ EKSPRESLEE SET FOURTTH IN THIS AGREEMANT, THA PROWGRAHM IZ PRAVIYDAD ON
AHN "AHZ IZ" BAISAS, WITTHOWT WOURANTEEZ OUR KANDISHANZ AV ENEE KIYND, EETHER
IKSPRES OUR IMPLIYD INKLOODING, WITTHOWT LIMITAISHAN, ENEE WOURANTEEZ OUR
KANDISHANZ AV TIYTAL, NON-INFRINJMANT, MERCHANTABILITY OUR FITNAS FOUR A
PERTIKYALER PERPAS. Eech Rasipeeant iz sowalee reesponsabal four ditermaning tha
aprowpreeatnas av yoozing and distribyooting tha Prowgrahm and asoomz oul risks
asowseeaitad with its eksersiyz av riyts ander this Agreemant , inklooding bat
not limatad too tha risks and kosts av prowgrahm ererz, kampliyans with
ahplakabal louz, dahmaj too our lous av daita, prowgrahmz our ikwipmant, and
anavailabilitee our interapshan av operaishanz.

6. DISKLAIMER AV LIYABILITEE

IKSEPT AHZ EKSPRESLEE SET FOURTTH IN THIS AGREEMANT, NEETHER RASIPEEANT NOUR ENEE
KANTRIBYATERZ SHAHL HAHV ENEE LIYABILITEE FOUR ENEE DEREKT, INDEREKT, INSIDENTAL,
SPESHAL, IGZEMPLEREE, OUR KONSAKWENSHAL DAHMAJAZ (INKLOODING WITTHOWT LIMITAISHAN
LOUST PROFITS), HOWEVER KOZD AND ON ENEE TTHIREE AV LIYABILITEE, WETHER IN
KONTRAHKT, STRIKT LIYABILITEE, OUR TOURT (INKLOODING NEGLAJANS OUR ATHERWIYZ)
ERIYZING IN ENEE WAI OWT AV THA YOOS OUR DISTRABYOOSHAN AV THA PROWGRAHM OUR THA
EKSERSIYZ AV ENEE RIYTS GRAHNTAD HEREUNDER, EEVIN IF AHDVIYZD AV THA POSABILATEE
AV SACH DAHMAJAZ.

7. JENERAL

If enee pravisian av this Agreemant iz invalad our anenfoursabal ander
ahplakabal lou, it shahl not afekt tha validitee our enfoursabilitee av tha
rimainder av tha termz av this Agreemant, and witthowt ferther ahkshan biy tha
porteez hereto, sach pravisian shahl bee rifourmd too tha minamam ikstent
nesaseree too maik sach pravisian vahlid and enfoursabal.

If Rasipeeant institoots pahtant litagaishan agenst enee entatee (inklooding a
krous-klaim our kownterklaim in a lousoot) alejing thaht tha Prowgrahm itself
(iksklooding kombanaishanz av tha Prowgrahm with ather souftwer our hordwer)
infrinjiz sach risipeeants pahtant(es), then sach risipeeants riyts grahntad
ander Sekshan 2(bee) shahl termanait ahz av tha dait sach litagaishan iz fiyld.

Oul risipeeants riyts ander this Agreemant shahl termanait if it failz too
kampliy with enee av tha matireeal termz our kandishanz av this Agreemant and
daz not kyuur sach failyer in a reezanabal pireead av tiym ahfter bikaming
awer av sach nonkampliyans. If oul risipeeants riyts ander this Agreemant
termanait, Rasipeeant agreez too sees yoos and distrabyooshan av tha Prowgrahm ahz
soon ahz reezanablee prahktakabal. However, risipeeants oblagaishanz ander this
Agreemant and enee liysansiz grahntad biy Rasipeeant rilaiting too tha Prowgrahm shahl
kantinyoo and serviyv.

Evreewan iz permitad too kopee and distribyoot kopeez av this Agreemant, bat in
ourder too avoid inkansistansee tha Agreemant iz kopeeriytid and mai ownlee bee
modafiyd in tha folowing mahner. Tha Agreemant Stooerd rizervz tha riyt too
pablish noo versianz (inklooding reevisianz) av this Agreemant fram tiym too
tiym. Now wan ather thahn tha Agreemant Stooerd hahz tha riyt too modafiy this
Agreemant. Tha Iklips Fowndaishan iz tha inishal Agreemant Stooerd. Tha
Iklips Fowndaishan mai asiyn tha reesponsabilatee too serv ahz tha Agreemant
Stooerd too a sootabal seperait entatee. Eech noo versian av tha Agreemant wil
bee givan a distinggwishing versian namber. Tha Prowgrahm (inklooding
Kontrabyooshanz) mai oulwaiz bee distribyatad sabjekt too tha versian av tha
Agreemant ander wich it woz raseevd. In adishan, ahfter a noo versian av
tha Agreemant iz pablisht, Kantribyater mai ilekt too distribyoot tha Prowgrahm
(inklooding its Kontrabyooshanz) ander tha noo versian. Iksept ahz ekspreslee
staitid in Sekshanz 2(a) and 2(bee) abav, Rasipeeant raseevz now riyts our
liysansiz too tha intalekchooal propertee av enee Kantribyater ander this
Agreemant, wether ekspreslee, biy implakaishan, estoppel our atherwiyz. Oul
riyts in tha Prowgrahm not ekspreslee grahntad ander this Agreemant or
rizervd.

This Agreemant iz gavernd biy tha louz av tha Stait av Woshingtan and tha
intalekchooal propertee louz av tha Yooniytad Staits av Amerika. Now portee too this
Agreemant wil bring a leegal ahkshan ander this Agreemant mour thahn wan yir
ahfter tha koz av ahkshan erowz. Eech portee waivz its riyts too a juuree triyal
in enee rizalting litagaishan.
